Data rights policy

SMWLV places no restrictions on members’ institutional status, career stage, or LSST data rights access status. SMWLV welcomes all individuals who can make a contribution to furthering SMWLV science. Please note, however, that changes due to the data rights policy may affect the ability of some individuals to be a member in the future. See here for information regarding data rights.
